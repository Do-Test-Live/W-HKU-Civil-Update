STRATEGIC RESEARCH FOCUSES

  • Low Carbon
    • Net zero and carbon neutrality
    • Zero carbon building
    • Low carbon / energy infrastructure
    • Sustainable refurbishment
    • Carbon auditing and life cycle assessment
    • Building and infrastructure energy performance appraisal
    • Human behaviour in carbon emissions and energy consumption
  • Project Delivery
    • New Engineering Contract
    • Off-site prefabrication, modular integrated construction (MiC)
    • Carbon / energy driven project delivery approaches
    • Subcontractor management
    • Supply chain management and integration
    • Productivity and Key performance indicators
  • Construction Informatics
    • Building information modelling (BIM)
    • Sustainable information modelling
    • Radio frequency identification (RFID) application
    • Smart metering and energy monitoring devices
    • Integrative BIM-enabled management system
    • BIM GIS integrated parametric analysis and diagnosis
    • Digital Twin
    • Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Robotics
  • Emerging Topics
    • Smart city
    • Infrastructure resilience

The Centre for Innovation in Construction & Infrastructure Development (CICID), which is in the Department of Civil Engineering of The University of Hong Kong, was established in November 2002.
